Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Elite Estates

Listing a property is simple. Create an account as an agent or agency, complete your profile verification, and use the "Add Property" option in your dashboard. Fill in property details, upload high-quality images, set your price, and publish. Your listing will go live after a quick review by our team to ensure quality standards.

Click "Sign In" in the top navigation and select "Register as Agent." Fill in your personal details, upload your real estate license, provide references, and submit. Our team will verify your credentials within 24–48 hours. Once approved, you can create a professional profile, list properties, and connect with potential buyers directly.

We offer flexible plans: a Free Plan with up to 3 listings, a Professional Plan at PKR 1,500/month for 15 listings with featured badges, and an Agency Plan at PKR 5,000/month for unlimited listings, priority support, and analytics. All plans include WhatsApp inquiry integration and basic property management tools.

Payments are processed securely through our integrated payment gateway. We accept debit/credit cards, JazzCash, Easypaisa, and bank transfers. Subscriptions auto-renew monthly but you can cancel anytime from your dashboard. Invoices and receipts are generated automatically for your records.

Use the search bar on the homepage or go to the Properties page. You can filter by city, property type (house, apartment, plot, commercial), price range, bedrooms, and listing status (for sale or rent). You can also browse featured properties and view detailed pages with photos, specs, and agent contact information.

On any property detail page, click the "Schedule Viewing" button or contact the listing agent directly via WhatsApp, phone, or the contact form. You can discuss available time slots directly with the agent. We recommend confirming your appointment at least 24 hours in advance. Viewings are free of charge.

While we are not a direct lender, we provide a built-in Mortgage Calculator to help you estimate monthly payments. We also connect you with partner banks and financing institutions that offer home loans, Islamic financing, and installment plans. Speak to one of our agents for personalized guidance on financing options.

Typical documents include: Computerized National Identity Card (CNIC) of buyer and seller, property title deed (Fard), mutation documents, tax receipts, power of attorney (if applicable), sale agreement, and NOC from the housing society.
